Acceditation And Registration

Richfiled Graduate Institute of Technology (PTY) Ltd is accredited by the Council on Higher Education (CHE), registered with the Department of Higher Education & Training as a Private Higher Education Institution under the Higher Education Act, 1997, Registration Certificate No. 2000/HE07/008. Higher Eduction Programmes are offered at Higher Certificate, Advanced Certificate, Diploma, Degree, Postgratuate Diploma, Honours and Masters level in the Faculties of Information and Communication Technology; Business, Economics and Management Sciences. The institution has a proud track record of 33 years in operation with 140 000 students having completed a range of programmes over this period.

Libracy and E-Library

The Fourth Industrail Revolution is the current and developing environment in which disruptive technologies and trends such as the Internet of Things (IoT), robotics, virtual reality (VR) and artificial intelligence (AI) are changing the way libraries work. The institution has taken cognizance of the important role of technology in bibraries and has enhanced the libraries to cater for advancing technology and research in all its campuses. Students have the added advantage of accessing e-libraries irrespective of where they are, empowering them to draw from a wider range of research matrial globally. Products of digital resources have been purchased with the aim to create a modern, accomplished, user-friendly and simple to use electronic library. The Institution has partnered with some of the world's greatest academic digital publishers such as EBSCO, ELsevier ScienceDirect, Emerald Group, Spring Nature and PressReader to provide free access to a range of e-newspapers, e-magazines, e-boks, e-cases and e-journals to its students. The campus library consists not only prescribed textbooks but textbooks of many categories.
The Annual Academic Library Conference is held virtually.
The conference is held to create an opportunioty for acedemic book publihers, academics and librarians to engage on significant issues in support of quality library, information services and librarianship nationally.
